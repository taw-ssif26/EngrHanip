About

Trained on codes. Proven on sites.

I started as a site engineer pouring concrete in Chattogram's monsoon season, before moving into structural design for mid- and high-rise developments. That order matters — I design for what actually happens on a construction site, not just what passes an ETABS check.

Diploma in Civil Engineering, P.Eng. licensed, and lead structural designer on projects ranging from 12-storey residential towers to a 40m-span industrial warehouse for a garment manufacturer. I run every project through the same discipline: survey, model, analyze, document, supervise.
